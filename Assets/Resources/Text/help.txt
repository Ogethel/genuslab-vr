GenusView (v0.3 alpha) by David Dumas

Arrows/WASD = Walk around
Space = Toggle mode WALK/DRAW
Left Button or Left Shift = Hold to draw (when in DRAW mode)
Right Button or Left Alt = Hold to GRAB surface
Z = Clear drawing
K = Toggle Poincare/Klein model
H = Toggle this help screen
Escape = Quit

Mouse controls view direction (in WALK mode), laser position (in DRAW mode), or rotation of the surface (in GRAB mode).

Press H again to hide the help screen.
