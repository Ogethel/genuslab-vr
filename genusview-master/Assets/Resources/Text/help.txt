GenusView (v0.4 alpha) by David Dumas

Arrows/WASD = Walk around
Space = Toggle mode WALK/DRAW
Left Button or Shift = Hold to draw (when in DRAW mode)
Right Button or Alt = Hold to GRAB surface
Z = Clear drawing
P = Toggle Poincare/Klein model
I,J,K,L = Move H^2 view
O = Reset H^2 view
H = Toggle this help screen
Escape = Exit help screen
Control-Q = Quit

Mouse controls view direction (in WALK mode), laser position (in DRAW mode), or rotation of the surface (in GRAB mode).

Press H or Escape to hide the help screen.
